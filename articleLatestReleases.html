Latest Pokemon Card Releases

Scarlet & Violet

Released on November 18, 2022, the Scarlet & Violet expansion introduces new Pokémon, mechanics, and cards to the Pokémon TCG. This set features Pokémon from the Paldea region and includes powerful new cards that can shake up the competitive scene.

Paldea Evolved

Released on September 22, 2023, the Paldea Evolved expansion builds upon the Scarlet & Violet set by introducing new Pokémon and powerful cards. This set continues to explore the Paldea region and adds exciting new strategies for players to discover.

Obsidian Flames

Released on June 7, 2024, the Obsidian Flames expansion is the latest addition to the Pokémon TCG. This set features new Pokémon and cards that bring fresh strategies and excitement to the game. Players can look forward to discovering what this new expansion has in store!
