Latest Pokemon Card Releases

Mega Evolution Base Set

Released on September 26th, 2025, Mega Evolution Base Set reintroduces a past mechanic of the game, Mega Evolutions! These large HP Pokemon pack a punch and can be the backbone of a competive deck, but becareful, because these Mega Pokemon give up 3 prize cards when knocked out!

Mega Evolution: Phantasmal Flames

Released on November 14th, 2025, Phantasmal Flames brings a fiery fan favorite back into the new expansion of the Pokemon TCG. Mega Charizard is back! Pair the original fire starter with support cards like Oricorio ex to unleash a powerful fire attack on your opponents!

Mega Evolution: Perfect Order

Released on March 27th, 2026, Perfect Order features a brand new fighting Mega. Mega Zygarde from Pokemon Legends ZA gets it's first card in the TCG! Pair this fiesty fighting type with all new support cards like Barbaracle and Tarragon to wipe your opponents board clean!
